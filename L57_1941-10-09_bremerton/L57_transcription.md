Friday Nite Oct 10
                                                          U.S.S. New Orleans
                                                          % Postmaster
                                                          Bremerton, Wash.
                                                          F Division

I.

Joan, Dearest;

Well your letter came yesterday and finally have time to answer it tonite. I read your letters over & over until I get the next one. You awful sweet Joan to take the trouble writting to me so much. You never know how much I appreciate it either. If you keep on you will make me think you really like me. I believed you care for me, anyway and I think you are the sweetest girl in this whole wide world.

Just got a letter from Carroll today and said the operation were comming along fine. Guess he is at home by now. Said him and Martha would get to spend a few days at home. The rest (over)

(2.)

will probably do, Martha some good. Guess, Thomas will be kindly lonesome by hisself in Detroit while Carroll's away. Just got finished writting him a few words tonite. Looks as if they have him in the Army before long. Once they get him in he might as well plan to stay there until this war is over. Really hope he won't have to go, though. I know he wouldn't like the army at all for the navy bad enough to me. The army is just about four times worse.

Hope, you & Elizabeth McKinney have a grand week-end as you said she was going to spend this week-end with you. Guess it will be too cold for swimming though. I been going swimming once in awhile but they are in-door swimming pools.

(3.)

I bet the Kentucky moons are awful pretty now as they are kindly nice here. They aren't as lovely as the ones you have in Ky. They seem to be the prettiest this time of year there. Hangs so low in the sky and looks as if a person can reach up and take a hold of them. Remember the night I left how pretty it were? I never forget it. [[As I were taking the last look I said to myself this might be my last time to see it at all.]] Although I kept saying to myself that it wouldn't be long before I could come back agin. Now, every time I look at the moon I think about you & wondering what you would be doing at the time. Seems like we are so close together when I'm out (over)

(4.)

walking in the Moonlight. Maybe you think I'm crazy but I don't care. That's the way I feel about you & I hope & I believed you do know how I feel about you.

[[Joan, I give anything if you would only make me that promise to marry me when you finish school.]] I haven't much to [strikethrough: start] start with but I believed we could get along swell together. I can't ask you not to go out with others how when made that promise. It wouldn't be fair on your part. Just like giving some one money but told them they couldn't spend it, because you once own it. Can't you see what I mean. The way it is now you are free and do what ever you please. Maybe you aren't sure whether you love me enough. Why don't you think it over & let

(5)

me know for sure.

Joan, I saw two awful good shows the last two nights. If you can possible get to see them be sure and go for they are really good. One is "Manhunt" with Walter Pidgeon & Joan Bennett. The other is "Kiss The Boys Goodbye," with Mary Martin & Don Ameche. You will like them both.

Well Darling don't you think I said enough for once? Guess I said a little to much. Please write agin soon.

I'm sorry about Bill cutting his finger off. Hope it's getting along fine. Looking for a letter very soon. Be good.

                                                          Loads of Love,
                                                                  Always, "Gene"
