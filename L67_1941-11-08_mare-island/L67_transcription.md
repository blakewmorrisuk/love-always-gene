note: |
  The ship's screw broke down on the first day out from Long Beach. The New Orleans turned around and came back to Mare Island in San Francisco Bay for repairs. The Tuesday-night phone call from Long Beach was already history; Gene was now waiting in San Francisco for the letter Joan had written him with the yes, which he was sure was still in the mail. 'I felt like jumping up & down when you said over the phone that you had made that promise in your last letter.'

Guess you will surely be surprise to hear that I'm here in San Francisco tonight. Anyway I'm just as surprise as you are in hearing it. This is what happened to us & the only reason we are here. [[As you know we got under way for, Hawaii Thursday morning & the first day out one of our screws broke down. So we had to turn around & come up to San Francisco to get it repaired.]] Although it won't take them long to fix that. We probably if not sooner leave again Monday for Honolulu. I'm though, I'm really glad that we got to come to San Francisco for three days. Think this was about the only large town on the west coast that I had't been in. So I'm going in tomorrow & take in few of the sights. The day & night before I left, Long Beach the Tellner — boy, the guy you talked to over the phone, were in Los Angles, San Pedro & a few of the smaller towns. We had a nice time & saw about every thing worth seeing. They say there is quite a lot of pretty things here in San Francisco, to see. So I guess Parkhurst & myself will go in tomorrow & take in a few of them. Joan, I saving postcards of different places & things I have seen. I'll send you some if you care to see them. I guess you are tired of reading about So let's talk about something else a while. I didn't get that letter before I left, Long Beach (the one you said you had change your mind). I thought be sent on up here, though. You know Parkhurst were planning on being with me the night I phoned you, but he had the duty & couldn't leave the ship. I told him the next morning about you making me that promise. He said (over) couldn't do that to him and he is and get marry just as soon as I do. You not sorry you made that promise are you? I'm really glad, Darling & I believe you are too. [[I felt like jumping up & down when you said over the phone that you had made that promise in your last letter.]] I just can't hardly wait to get that letter.

Darling, I hope you don't care for me writing this letter in pencil. Parkhurst has got my pen & he is gone up to the movies. So I thought the pencil would be just as good. I doubt if you can read my letters anyway. I can't sometimes myself. I got a letter from Mother & also one from Martha the morning I left Long Beach.

I guess you are tired of this letter by now. I'll have to bring it to a close anyway as it's getting late. Joan, when you answer send the letter to, Honolulu, as I probably be on my way there by the time you get this letter. We both fell out & I were halfway crying the whole morning we left Long Beach. I wasn't the only one either as the whole bunch felt blue. The worst part we got to go through the whole thing again. Be good Darling, I dream about me writing —
