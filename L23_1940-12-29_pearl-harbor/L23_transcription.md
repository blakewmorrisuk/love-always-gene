Sunday, Dec. 29
U.S.S. New Orleans
Pearl Harbor, Honolulu T.H.
F. Division

Dearest Joan,

Thought I have forgotten all about you by now I guess. Well, I haven't by a long shot yet, and I can't think of any good reason why I haven't been writing if it isn't this lazy bug of mine. You wouldn't believe anyway, so why should I [?] some of it. I got xmas card and also the xmas gift and I'm really proud of it. I guess you think I'm some guy forgetting all about [you], Joan, well I didn't forget at that. You will see what I mean one of these days.

I couldn't think of one [?] thing to send Joan that I thought you would want, so it's just a little to fair to send. That isn't will be [?] but it is just as good in love as fine words from, and you don't ask me write it [?] you would guess if you stop to think enough? I think you enjoyed it a lot. I received a letter from home a couple of days ago. I only wrote them one letter since the last time I wrote to you. I bet they think I'm dead. Hell, the time letter? - really gone at me. The thing they wouldn't understand things isn't so good with them. Dad said mother had to take some kind of stuff all the time for her side, I think it's the same trouble she had 3 [?] years ago. I also received a nice xmas card from Martha.

Well, Joan, you can talk about it being cold back there and all. The weather is still from 90 to 100 out here. It only drops down to about 80 at night. The sun shines right through you every day. It seems more like July there it does December.

Joan, if you really want a picture of me I will go over sometime this week and have one made, so don't blame me after you get it, for you asked for it. As you might say, I don't sit. My heart isn't in it.

Will have to bring this letter to a close but I will write again soon. I [am] sorry I have been so long but I promise it never happen again, and you know me to be (I think you understand). There isn't a day or night that goes by that I don't think about you. Remember the nights we were together? Will I think there might come more just as those if [?] later. Please keep on writing, Joan, and if I don't write as often as I should, forgive me. Be a good girl like you always have been and don't let some boy talk you into doing things that you shouldn't. Stay the same girl as I knew you. Things might be dull at times but after all it comes [in] write everybody life. So take is with a smile.

Write Soon,
With Lots of Love
Gene
