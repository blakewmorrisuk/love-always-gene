Well this is Friday night and thought I would write you a few lines. I haven't heard from you yet but guess it's because it hasn't been enough time. Sure hope this letter finds you & also rest of the family in the best of health. I'm good as usual myself and you might say the same old boy. Anyway I have been thinking a lot about you & you always will be on my mind.

I guess you & Peggy have had a swell week at school. Wished I was back and going to school with you. We could really have a good time together. Joan you really don't know what a good (over) time I had being with you and the folks at home for the short time I was there. Jack Parkhurst has gone on his leave now and bet that guy will never get back. I just sit around now and think about a lots of things and you know how blue a guy can get sometimes. [[I have wished a thousand of times that I never joined this navy.]] There isn't anything really I ever like about it at all.

Are you still going to take nurse training? You know what I want you to do. Guess though I not much to judge that.

Well Joan there isn't a bare thing around here to write. Haven't heard from home yet and sure would like to get a letter [strikethrough] from someone soon. Will have to write Martha a few words tonight also. Be sure to write Joan whenever you can get the chance and don't go and try to studing school books to hard. I am always thinking about you & wondering what you would be doing.

I guess I will have to make this letter short, but you can always look for another letter in a couple of days. Be a good girl Joan & I do the same. Looking for a letter soon.
