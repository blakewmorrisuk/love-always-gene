> Christmas greeting card, not a letter. Religious nativity-scene illustration on the front (shepherds with sheep beneath the Star of Bethlehem). Brief printed verse inside with Gene's handwritten signature.

**Envelope (IMG_9249):**
- Postmark: SAN DIEGO, CALIF. DEC 15 7PM 1940
- Cancellation slogan: "MAIL EARLY FOR CHRISTMAS"
- 3-cent stamp
- Addressed to: Miss Joan Northcutt / Stanford, Kentucky / Route 1

**Card front (IMG_9250):**
Color illustrated nativity scene — shepherds in red and yellow robes tending a flock of sheep; the Star of Bethlehem shines above; figures of the three Magi appear in silhouette in the distance.

**Card interior (IMG_9251) — printed verse:**
> May the Star that shone
> O'er Bethlehem
> That holy Christmas night,
> Its gentle radiance shed on you
> And make
> your Christmas bright

**Handwritten signature (Gene's hand):**
> Love,
> Gene.
