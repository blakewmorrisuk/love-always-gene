Will try to answer your letter tonight which I received this morning. I was glad to hear from you, but I believed there's another letter you wrote since this one that I haven't received yet. [[You were sarcastically about some thing in this letter. What's seem to be the matter now? Has some body been giving you the wrong information? Maybe it was something I said in one of my letters. If I did just don't think anything of it.]]

Well, I'm alright in about every way. Hope your family are the same. Just got an letter from home took and one from, Martha yesterday.

Joan, of course I still want the picture. Sorry you had to go in the trouble in getting a camera.

Darling, there's nothing I know to write. You said your self I probably don't have the time to write to you. [[Well I write more letters to Joan Northcutt than I ever dreamed of writing to anyone else. If you want to know personally how many other girls I write to just let me know. I have no secrets you know.]]

Yes I heard the songs you mention in the letter & like them very well. No I don't do much dancing or ever expect to. The only time I ever try to dance is when I'm drunk then I'm to drunk to do very good. You can have it, thanks.

Well will be looking for another letter soon. Be good. Bye —
