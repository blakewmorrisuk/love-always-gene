                                                          U.S.S. New Orleans
                                                          Pearl Harbor, Honolulu, T.H.
                                                          F Division

I.

Dearest Joan,

Received your letter about two days ago and sure glad to get the letter but didn't like the news about you being sick. I hope this letter finds you again in the best of health and also the rest of the family. Yes, Jo, I'm O.K. and haven't been sick a day since I have been out here. Haven't heard from home for over a week now. Did I tell you about Buster writting me a letter? Although he didn't write much but what he did write was nice.

Well Joan this place is as dull as it is back there in Ky. About the same thing going on every day. We had a little excitement the night before we came back in the harbor. About 9 O'clock that

II.

night we got a radio call that [[a big Army bomber had went down about where we were]]. In about two hours there were (6) or more ships there searching for it with searchlights and it was a thrill to see it. Well about 10 O'clock the next day we found them, five in the plane still afloat. The next day it came out in the papers that another bomber found it up in the mountains and didn't say [strikethrough] anything about the navy searching for them. That goes to show that a person can't believed what the papers say.

Yes, Jo, I had a good time on the camping trip. We could do anything we cared to, fish, swim and at night they have a dance or you could stay drunk if you cared to. Anyone that wanted to can go out there each week end

III.

if we are in port. I guess I will go out again before long. It's a good place to go for this ship is a little tiresome at times.

Well, Joan [[it won't be long before we being heading back to the states]] and it won't be too soon for me. Will be home to see you if I can get that leave. It would be nice to get away from this navy for a bit for about a month.

So you still like to know what your present is going to be. Why should I tell you? Anyway it's something nice and I think you will like it a lot. (I hope.)

Guess I have told you about every thing I know so will bring this letter to a close. Write to me when you get time and don't make the letters so short next time. Hope you are over

(Back of first page)
