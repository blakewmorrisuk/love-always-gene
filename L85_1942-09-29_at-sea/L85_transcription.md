[U.S.S. NEW ORLEANS — illustrated letterhead]
                                                          U.S.S. New Orleans
                                                          % Fleet Postmaster
                                                          San Francisco, Calif.
                                                          F Division

Dearest Joan,

Just got a letter from you today and one a couple of days ago. I'm so glad that you had finally received a letter from me. You are awfully nice to keep writting with out even hearing from me. If it weren't for your letters, Joan know what I would do. The folks home hardly ever write.

Joan the way you talked in your last letter about your grandmother she probably gone by now. Everybody got to face death sooner or later. It's something a person doesn't like to think of.

Well Darling finally I had that long promised picture made. Probably be

several days before I can get it, but I send it as soon I'm able. Though I know the thing won't be very good as I wasn't feeling very good the day I got it taken. Probably look just as bad as I felt.

Joan did I tell you that I received an letter from, J.B. Davidson awhile back? I was kindly surprised hearing from him. Said he was liking the country fine. [[Wished I could write and tell you the things I have seen. I can say I have been in, Australia and really loved the place. It's a beautiful country and the people there are so friendly. Sure had a nice time while I were there.]]

No Joan I'm not much at typing. It takes me to long to type a letter. You know I never did take it in school. I'm glad you like it though. I'm going to learn more

about it one of these days when I have more time.

[[Of course darling I still love you. Why you ask, I don't know. Yes, it was kindly queer the way we became engaged — wasn't it? I can't see anything wrong with it though.]]

Well guess I'm at the end of my rope as you say. Tell all I said hello. Write anytime it's possible darling as I'm always waiting for your letters. Will try to write agin soon. Be good.

                                                          Love & kisses
                                                              always "Gene"

(R.E. Lankford)
