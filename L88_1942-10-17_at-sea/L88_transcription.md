                                                          U.S.S. New Orleans
                                                          % Fleet Postmaster
                                                          San Francisco, Calif.
                                                          F Division

(1)

Dearest Joan;

Your sweet hour letter I received today and I know you wouldn't mind if I wrote a few lines tonite, Joan. It's so nice to get your letters for they sure helps me a lot. I hardly know what I do without them. I can always depend on a letter from you when every one else fails.

Darling I'm so happy that everything fine there. Myself I'm getting along very well but not so good in certain ways either. [[You know a person will get wires cross sometimes. Well, my seems to be crossed all the time and nothing I can do about it.]] So you are gaining weight. Yes I'm glad for I have always said it's best to gain weight then to be losing it.

(2)

Now don't get me wrong and get fat. I still weigh about the same as always 150 lbs. Guess I've done all the growing that I'm going to. Anyway I shan't be the tallest in the family. I know Carroll isn't any longer than I even though he thinks he is.

Darling I have finally sent you the picture that I been promising for so long. You probably got it by now as I sent it three days ago. Hope you like it which you probably won't. If I stop getting letters from you at least I know the reason why. Also I sent the folks one too. Will have to sent Martha one as she might feel hurt if I don't. Joan guess you are wondering what that picture of another sailor is doing with my. Well that sailor, Don Chapin one of my

(3)

best friends. In fact my best friend I have now. The girl is his wife, Betty and we are friends too. Although it has been a long time since I last saw her. I think she's kindly nice looking and a lot of fun. Her home town is, Seattle, Wash. Joan, keep that picture for me for that's the only one I have and I had a hard time getting that one.

Well darling as you know there's nothing interesting I can write. Guess old, Carroll is liking the navy by now. I feel sorry for him if he doesn't. Yes I sure hope we can see each other soon. Hoping is about all a person can do though.

[[To tell the truth I am a little low in faith Darling, but as you said guess you have enough for us both.]] I do miss you more every day. Don't worry I never forget you. There's isn't a girl who can ever yet would take your place. Be good & Bye.

                                                          Lots of Love & Kisses
                                                              Yours "Gene"

(R.E. Lankford)
