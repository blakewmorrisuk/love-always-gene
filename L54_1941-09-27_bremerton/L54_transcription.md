Your letter came a few minutes ago and will try to answer it right now. No kidding, Joan your letters makes me feel so much better after reading them. I sure hope you like to write to me as much, or even half as much as I love writting to you.

Joan, you sure have been having a hard time, the way you talk. Stomach ache and a swollen up ankle & may be a broken neck by now. How in the devil can you have so many things happen to you Sweetheart. You have better learn to take more care of yourself. (over) The little cold that I had is about all gone now. Might have another in a day or so though.

Believe me or not, Joan I'm really getting to be a good boy. I have been going to church every Sunday night and sometime twice a week. Haven't had a drink of anything since I been back, not even beer for the looks of it makes me sick.

I want to go to the big foot-ball game at Seattle tomorrow for Wash. [strikethrough: State] and Minn. are playing. I have the week end duty and don't know though if I be able to get some one to take it for me. Guess your uncle Bill will be listening to it over the radio. Seattle is just across the bay from here. Bremerton is a large town it self. Around 80 thousand people I think. All I have to do is step off the ship and walk about two hundred yards and be right on main street of town. I'm going to kindly hate leaving this place. You may talk about how you like going to Honolulu and I would like to also if I hadn't already been there once. You know I spent one long year out there and saw about everything worth seeing. So why would I want to go back agin? Any way that is a awful damn long ways from home.

So Highland has got a good soft ball team this year (over) Wished I was going to school up there and could play soft ball & basket ball with them. Who said I would be surprised about you being the prettiest girl there? Guess I know when I see and pick a sweet girl like you, may be, it's going to be hard for me to hold you. Being away from you like this isn't so good you know. I feel sorry for the boy that even look like he might want to double cross me. That is if I found out about it which I could you know. Huh!

Well, Honey really there isn't anything to write about around here. Just a lots of noise and so on. Sometimes I say if I ever get back to the country where it's peaceful & quiet I will never want to leave it agin. [[Guess I'm nothing but a country boy at heart.]]

Darling, write soon and I be seeing you in my dreams too. Take it easy on those Highland Heifers for they are just kidding you to have a little fun. You know who I crazy about and always will. Good-bye, until the next letter.
