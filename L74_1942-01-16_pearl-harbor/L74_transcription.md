  Five weeks after Pearl Harbor. Received four letters from you since yesterday. So maybe I had better write one anyway. I think your mail was held here in the states. So you see why I hadn't gotten it sooner. It's been impossible for me to write any sooner. You know I will write as often as I can. May be that all I may will make up for the other part. Sure I believed it will. Anyhow I will be able to write more often now.

Sure surprise to hear about Martha being married. I thought it would. But though. That goes to show how quick a girl can change her mind. Sometimes I wonder. Now don't get me wrong Joan. I haven't received that picture yet or either the Xmas present & you said something about sending. Guess what, I also got a letter from Louise Dunagan yesterday. Seems like her & Carroll aren't getting along so good lately. You know how Carroll is at times. All the Lankfords aren't that way though. [[You asked if the promise still stands between you & I. Of course it does & always will for my part. So don't get any foolish ideals in your head.]]

The weather really must be awful cold there the way you talk. It's a little cool here but not to cold.

Joan, I would like to see you just as much you like to see me. If I was back there now I probably would freeze. He Joan, [[I still want to send that ring if you want it. Will you please send me the size? Let me know soon.]]

Well, Darling there isn't much more I know to write. I will be looking for a long letter soon. Keep writting every chance you have & I do the same. Be good & take care of yourself. Looking for an answer soon. Tell everyone I said hello, will you?
